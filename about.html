Hello I'm Tomasz

A devoted father of two and an aspiring web developer passionate about crafting engaging user experiences. My journey into web development was sparked by curiosity and a drive to enhance my online shop, leading me down a path of self-directed learning on platforms like Codecademy, Front End Masters and Udemy. This journey was further enriched by participating in government-sponsored coding bootcamps. Juggling family, work and studies has been a formidable challenge, yet it has offered me the time to thoroughly grasp the fundaments of web programming.
Away from the keyboard, I can be found at my local gym or enjoying quality time with family and friends. I really enjoy exploring new places and meeting new people.

If my story resonates with you, I would be delighted to discuss how I can contribute to your projects and support the growth of your organisation.
